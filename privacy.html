Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

Your learning data is yours. We use it to optimize your experience—never to sell, never to exploit. Period.

What We Collect

  • Learning behavior: How you interact with content, when breakthroughs happen, where you struggle
  • Account information: Name, email, payment details (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Usage data: Session times, feature usage, device information

How We Use It

  • Personalization: Adapt learning paths to your unique patterns
  • Improvement: Make the system better for everyone (aggregated, anonymized)
  • Communication: Send you updates about your learning progress

What We Don't Do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your learning patterns without consent
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track you across other websites

Your Rights

You can:

  • Export all your data at any time
  • Delete your account and data permanently
  • Opt out of aggregated research use
  • Control what data educators can see (if applicable)

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular security audits. Your learning data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Legal Compliance

GDPR (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed (we don't sell)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Children's Privacy

For users under 13, we require parental consent and collect only minimal necessary data. Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized aggregate data for research and service improvement.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Questions?

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at: support@synapate.com
